ChemBio Hub

Chemical biology across the University of Oxford

Monday 10th November 2014
Saïd Business School
09:00 - 17:20

A symposium by ChemBio Hub to strengthen interdepartmental research connections

  • Overview
  • Registration
  • Programme

Speakers from eight Oxford departments will explore a diverse range of topics, with a particular focus on interdepartmental collaboration in small molecule drug discovery.

Attend to find out more about who works on what, and where. Meet new colleagues from across the University and explore ideas for new research connections.

This event is generously supported by:
