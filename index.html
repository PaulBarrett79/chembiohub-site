OOMMPPAA - directed synthesis and data analysis tool

OOMMPPAA is a novel computational tool designed to inform compound design by combining structural and activity data.

Citation: Anthony R. Bradley, Ian D. Wall, Darren V.S. Green, Charlotte M. Deane, Brian D. Marsden (2014). OOMMPPAA: A tool to aid directed synthesis by the combined analysis of activity and structural data
